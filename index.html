Rules

One player

  • Roll the dice
  • If any number from 2 - 6 is rolled, then this number is added to your score
  • If a 1 is rolled, you lose and your score is reset
  • Win by achieving a score of 20 or higher

Two Players

  • Player One starts and can roll the dice
  • If any number from 2 - 6 is rolled, then this number is added to your score this turn
  • If a 1 is rolled, their score they accumulated this turn is lost and their turn ends
  • The player can press "End Turn" to end their turn, banking their score this turn into their total score and ending their turn
  • A player wins by achieving a score of 20 or higher
